## A. Delight & Micro-Interactions
1. **Cursor trail embers** — Extend ember particles so faint sparks trail the cursor site-wide
2. **Typewriter chatbot streaming** — AI chat responses stream character-by-character with blinking cursor
3. **Haptic button feedback** — All buttons scale down 2% on press then spring back
4. **3D card tilt on hover** — Cards follow cursor with subtle rotateX/Y perspective effect
5. **Animated theme transition** — Sun/moon morph with radial color sweep on toggle
6. **Confetti on form submission** — Burst on successful contact form or newsletter submit
7. **Text scramble effect** — Headlines briefly cycle random characters before resolving
